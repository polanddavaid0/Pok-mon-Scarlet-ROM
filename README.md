Unlike previous Pokémon titles, Pokémon Scarlet features a fully open-world experience, allowing players to explore the region freely, complete objectives in any order, and follow multiple story paths.

## Key Features

### Open-World Exploration
- Explore the expansive Paldea region without traditional route restrictions.
- Travel across diverse environments including cities, mountains, lakes, deserts, and grasslands.
- Discover Pokémon roaming naturally throughout the world.
### Multiple Story Paths
Players can progress through three major storylines:
1. **Victory Road** – Challenge Gym Leaders and become a Pokémon Champion.
2. **Path of Legends** – Battle powerful Titan Pokémon.
3. **Starfall Street** – Confront Team Star and uncover their story.

### Legendary Pokémon
- Ride **Koraidon**, the Legendary Pokémon exclusive to Pokémon Scarlet.
- Unlock traversal abilities for land, water, and air exploration. 

### New Starter Pokémon
Choose your first partner Pokémon:
- Sprigatito (Grass)
- Fuecoco (Fire)
- Quaxly (Water) 
### Terastal Phenomenon
- Transform Pokémon through Terastallization.
- Change a Pokémon's type based on its Tera Type.
- Gain strategic advantages in battles.

### Multiplayer Features
- Play cooperatively with up to four players online.
- Explore Paldea together, participate in battles, and complete raids.
